# Ad Scraper Overview

## What is Ad Scraper?

Ad Scraper is a tool that helps you find and save high-performing ads and viral content from social media platforms. Use it to:

- **Research competitors** - See what ads your competitors are running
- **Find viral content** - Discover trending videos and posts in your niche
- **Save winning creatives** - Build a library of ads that work
- **Generate new ads** - Use scraped content as inspiration for new creatives
- **Detect winners** - AI identifies ads running 14+ days with strong signals
- **Estimate spend** - See how much competitors are spending on each ad
- **Track competitors** - Monitor specific advertisers over time

## Supported Platforms

| Platform | What You Get | Best For |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| **TikTok** | Viral videos, creator stats | Finding trending hooks and formats |
| **Instagram** | Posts, Reels, carousels | Creator content and visual inspiration |
| **Meta Ads Library** | Actual paid ads from FB/IG | Competitor ad research |

## How It Works

1. **Start a Scrape** - Choose a platform and enter a search term
2. **Wait for Results** - The scraper runs in the background (1-15 minutes)
3. **Browse Your Library** - View, filter, and analyze the scraped content
4. **Generate New Ads** - Use the content as inspiration for your own creatives
