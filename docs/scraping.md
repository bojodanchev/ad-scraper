# Scraping Guide

## Starting a New Scrape

### Step 1: Go to New Scrape

Click **"New Scrape"** in the navigation bar, or the button on the dashboard.

### Step 2: Choose Your Platform

Select one of:
- **TikTok** - For viral videos and trending content
- **Instagram** - For posts, Reels, and visual content
- **Meta Ads Library** - For competitor paid ads

### Step 3: Choose Search Type

| Search Type | Use For |
|-------------|---------|
| **Keyword** | General search (recommended) |
| **Hashtag** | Specific hashtag content |
| **Profile** | All content from one creator/page |

### Step 4: Enter Your Search

Type your search term. Examples:
- "AI automation" (keyword)
- "dropshipping" (hashtag, without #)
- "garyvee" (profile, without @)

### Step 5: Set Filters (Optional)

Customize your results with filters like:
- Time period (last 7 days, 30 days, etc.)
- Engagement minimums
- Creator follower ranges

### Step 6: Start Scrape

Click **"Start Scrape"** and wait. The scrape runs in the background.

## Viewing Results

### Ad Library

Once a scrape completes, results appear in your **Library**:

1. Go to **Library** in the navigation
2. Use filters to find specific content:
   - Platform (TikTok, Instagram, Meta)
   - Media type (Video, Image, Carousel)
   - Time period
   - Search by keyword

### What You Can Do With Results

- **View details** - Click any ad to see full info
- **Watch videos** - Play videos directly in the app
- **Copy URLs** - Get direct links to content
- **Analyze** - Run AI analysis on ads (coming soon)
- **Generate** - Use as inspiration for new creatives

## Best Practices

### For Finding Viral Content

1. **Search your niche keywords** - "AI automation", "ecommerce tips", etc.
2. **Sort by popular** - See what's actually going viral
3. **Check engagement** - High likes/views = winning content
4. **Study the hooks** - First 3 seconds matter most

### For Competitor Research

1. **Search competitor names** in Meta Ads Library
2. **Filter to active ads** - See what they're running NOW
3. **Check days running** - 30+ days = likely profitable
4. **Note the patterns** - What headlines/CTAs do they use?

### For Content Inspiration

1. **Scrape multiple hashtags** in your niche
2. **Mix platforms** - TikTok for hooks, Instagram for visuals
3. **Track creators** - Follow top performers in your space
4. **Save winners** - Build your swipe file

## Scrape Limits

| Setting | Limit | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Max items per scrape | 200 | Higher = longer scrape time |
| Concurrent scrapes | 3 | Wait for one to finish before starting another |
| Data retention | 7 days | Apify data expires after 7 days |
